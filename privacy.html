Privacy

We care about your privacy

Last updated: 03 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how wlcomputerhelp.co.uk (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you visit and use our website hosted using Cloudflare Pages and managed through GitHub.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from visitors unless you voluntarily provide it to us, such as through contacting us for support or communication purposes. In the event of a communication or support request, we may use the information you provide in order to reasonably carry out the work, assistance, or response required. Technical information may also be automatically collected by our hosting and infrastructure providers, including: - IP address - Browser type and version - Device information - Pages visited - Date and time of requests - Basic diagnostic and performance data

2. Hosting and Infrastructure

Our website is hosted using Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process technical and usage data necessary to deliver website content, improve performance, and protect against malicious activity. Source code and version control for this website are managed through GitHub.

3. Cookies

This website may use essential cookies or similar technologies required for security, performance, and functionality. We do not use cookies for advertising or profiling unless otherwise stated.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services or embedded content in the future. These services may independently collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the website and its infrastructure, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to: - Access your data - Request correction or deletion - Object to certain processing activities

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
